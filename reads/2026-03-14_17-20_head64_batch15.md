# Experiment Summary

## Hypothesis
Combining HEAD_DIM=64 with TOTAL_BATCH_SIZE=2^15 to get even more steps while maintaining more attention heads.

## Change Made
- HEAD_DIM = 64 (from 128)
- TOTAL_BATCH_SIZE = 2**15 (from 2**16)

This gives 8 attention heads and ~1066 steps in 15 minutes.

## Result
- val_bpb: 0.460609 (previous best: 0.464691) - **0.9% improvement**
- training_seconds: ~900
- peak_vram_mb: 1777.1 (same)
- num_steps: ~1066 (doubled from 533)
- n_head: 8

## Expected?
Yes - more steps with more heads should help.

## Decision
- Keep - new best!
