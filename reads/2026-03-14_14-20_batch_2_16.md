# Experiment Summary

## Hypothesis
Further reducing TOTAL_BATCH_SIZE from 2^17 to 2^16 would allow even more training steps within the 15-minute time budget, leading to better convergence.

## Change Made
Changed `TOTAL_BATCH_SIZE = 2**17` to `TOTAL_BATCH_SIZE = 2**16` in train.py. This reduces gradient accumulation steps from 16 to 8, approximately doubling the number of training steps again.

## Result
- val_bpb: 0.465289 (previous best: 0.481693) - **3% improvement**
- training_seconds: ~900
- peak_vram_mb: 1777.1 (same as baseline)
- num_steps: 533 (previous: 274) - **95% more steps**
- num_params_M: 50.3 (same model)

## Expected?
Yes - more steps led to better convergence.

## Decision
- Keep
